OUR PHILOSOPHY
Honest food. Extraordinary craft.
Ember & Oak was born from a simple conviction — that the best meals come from restraint, not extravagance. We source from farms within 100 miles, let the seasons write our menu, and cook everything over wood and coal.
Our chef, Marco Silva, trained across kitchens in Porto, Lyon, and San Sebastián before returning home to build something that felt true — a place that earns its place at the table every single night.
— Marco Silva, Head Chef
